Studio

Active in the soundtrack industry since 2007, Leonardo Padovani composes and records music using violin, classical viola (bowed), vocals, whistling, and the erhu — a traditional two-stringed Chinese instrument. A trusted collaborator for numerous audio production studios, he is known for his musical versatility and creative approach. In addition to performing solos, Padovani specializes in layered string arrangements — his own or those of other composers — expertly overdubbing multiple violin and viola parts to create rich, cinematic textures. His portfolio includes original music for television, film, advertising, theater, and wedding videos.

Atuante no mercado de trilhas sonoras desde 2007, Leonardo Padovani compõe e grava músicas utilizando violino, viola clássica (de arco), voz, assobios e o erhu — um instrumento chinês tradicional de duas cordas. Colaborador de confiança de diversos estúdios de produção de áudio, é reconhecido por sua versatilidade musical e abordagem criativa. Além das performances solo, Padovani é especializado em arranjos de cordas sobrepostos — tanto de sua autoria quanto de outros compositores — gravando múltiplas camadas de violinos e violas para criar texturas ricas e cinematográficas. Seu portfólio inclui músicas originais para televisão, cinema, publicidade, teatro e vídeos de casamento.

Activo en la industria de bandas sonoras desde 2007, Leonardo Padovani compone y graba música utilizando violín, viola clásica (con arco), voz, silbidos y el erhu, un instrumento tradicional chino de dos cuerdas. Colaborador de confianza de numerosos estudios de producción de audio, es reconocido por su versatilidad musical y su enfoque creativo. Además de interpretar solos, Padovani se especializa en arreglos de cuerdas superpuestos — propios o de otros compositores — grabando múltiples capas de violines y violas para crear texturas ricas y cinematográficas. Su portafolio incluye música original para televisión, cine, publicidad, teatro y videos de bodas.

Live Violin

Blending musical virtuosity with the energy of the dance floor, Leonardo Padovani delivers a dynamic live violin performance that elevates any celebration. Accompanied by a DJ, he improvises over the tracks in real time using advanced violin techniques and a guitar-style effects pedalboard, creating a truly electrifying experience. From weddings and milestone birthdays to corporate events and private parties, his performances captivate guests from the first note. Starting beside the DJ with a striking stage presence, Padovani soon joins the crowd, interacting with guests and transforming the dance floor into a vibrant, immersive show.

Weddings

With over 20 years of experience in the wedding industry, Leonardo Padovani brings a refined approach centered on elegance, simplicity, musical excellence, and a modern aesthetic. Each performance features a personalized repertoire that reflects the couple’s vision, infused with his distinctive style, adding depth and beauty to each moment. Padovani works closely with each couple to understand their expectations and guide the musical direction—from selecting instruments to preparing custom arrangements tailored to each moment of the ceremony. His performances are carefully crafted to enhance the emotion of every part of the celebration.
